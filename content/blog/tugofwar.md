At this after school program for children in a poor neighbourhood where children learn numeracy, art and yoga, we joined in to play tug of war one evening. <!--more--> First we made the rope, one that had to be really strong with 40 children and 5 adults could pull at each other. Here are some snapshots from that afternoon. At the end, we left with perspectives of working together to make a change just like it took water, soap, many hands and felting movements to change raw yarn into a very strong rope!!
